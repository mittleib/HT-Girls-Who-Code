FAQs

Where and when does the club meet?

Meetings are on Fridays from 3:05 pm to 4:05 pm in the Success Center (previously the computer lab) in the HT Upper School library.

Does the club have an admission fee?

No! Admission is completely free for all members.

Who can join?

EVERYONE! We welcome anyone of any gender identity, but be prepared to be in a 'sisterhood'.

Is this a strong commitment?

Only if you want it to be! There is no penalty for missing meetings. Programming in your free time or participating in special events outside of meetings isn't required, either. This club is an outlet for learning to code and building STEM projects among peers in a supportive environment. In other words, the club exists to benefit you, not to pressure you into committing time you don't have. But keep in mind, projects can be demanding, so if you attend more meetings you can participate more significantly in project building!

What should I bring?

A laptop or iPad and anything else you may want for brainstorming (like paper and a pencil). If you don't have an appropriate electronic device, we can provide you with one.
