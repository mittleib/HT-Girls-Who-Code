Our Calendar

*If an event is labeled as a "Universal GWC Event", then it is hosted by the head organization, Girls Who Code (not by our individual club). These universal events are for you to participate in on your own if you so choose. Our club is not associated with them. That being said, these events can be a great chance to learn more about using computer science in the real world or to have fun making projects with fellow girls who code!
